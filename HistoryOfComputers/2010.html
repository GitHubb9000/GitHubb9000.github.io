Technological advancements in the 2010s

The 2010s was a time of rapid technological progression and advancements.
Some key events that happened it the 2010s include:

4G

The widespread rollout of 4G promised up to 10 times existing speeds
and therfore super-fast downloads and on-the-go streaming.
This was a major catalyst to move past the era of "dumbphones"
and for people to move towards the era of "smartphones"

Smartphones

The release of 4g meant that smartphones became much more powerful and
useful. You could now enjoy services like Netflix while on-the-go.
This increase in usefulness meant that smartphones became extremely popular
very quickly, and therefore led us into the age mobile smartphones.

Cryptocurrency

The Cryptocurrency boom started in 2009 and then became a big thing
durng the 2010's and onwards. It all started with someone under the alias
of Satoshi Nakamoto announced a new peer-to-peer system called " Bitcoin"
This led to the creation of an entire ecosystem of virtual platforms
which revolutionised how people exchange money online.

A.I

The 2010s saw some major advancements in the A.I space. A.Is could now
intellingently detect human faces and identify who was in the photo using
just a small collection of samples images, which didn't have to be
professional photos and could just be photos taken on a phone
taken by anyone. During this time, OpenAI, the company who is
well known for creating ChatGPT, also created GPT - 1 ,
the ancestor to GPT- 3, which ChatGPT runs on. This led
to A.Is which could now talk naturally, just like a human.
