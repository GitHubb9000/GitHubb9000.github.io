Technological advancements in the 2010s

The 2010s was a time of rapid technological progression and advancements. Some key events that happened it the 2010s include:

4G

The widespread rollout of 4G promised up to 10 times existing speeds and therfore super-fast downloads and on-the-go streaming. This was a major catalyst to move past the era of "dumbphones" and for people to move towards the era of "smartphones"

Smartphones

The release of 4g meant that smartphones became much more powerful and useful. You could now enjoy services like Netflix while on-the-go. This increase in usefulness meant that smartphones became extremely popular very quickly, and therer for led us into the age mobile smartphones.
